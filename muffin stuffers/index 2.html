Death by Zip Code

By Sebastian and Lucas

Our data shows the different causes of death in various zip codes in California. Our data is taken throughout the time frame of 1999-2013. Our data shows us how these people died, what year they died, and what zip code they died in. Sebastian and I chose this project because be were both interested in health. When we saw this data, both of us were intrigued. We were interested in wether or not the same deaths are prevalent in the same zip codes. This could show how to reduce the amount of deaths in certain areas. This could be made possible by looking at the biggest causes for death in an area and the preventing that kind of death. In our data, we analyzed 5 deaths from each year in the same zip codes. Our data shows that the same deaths reoccur in the same place a lot. This shows that where you are, can determine how you die. In our data there is 13 different types of deaths. Out of the 13 deaths, SUI, or suicide, is the most prevalent type of death. In the zip code 90002 and 90008, suicide was almost every death, even throughout all 14 years. Therefore, these zip codes are not the nicest places to be."
