Mass Shootings Within the United States



Overview

This web page was designed to raise awareness about the increase in mass shootings across the US from 1995 to present day. The graphs are intended to convey the severity of these tragedies that some Americans have yet to grasp. We hope that through this web page, more individuals will become educated on this topic and take action to prevent further catastrophes.

Why?

Mass shootings have become increasingly prevalent as of the 21st century. Recently, on June 12, 2016, one of the worst mass shootings in US history occurred in Orlando, Florida; 49 lives were lost and 53 were wounded. This particular shooting is merely one of many shootings that has occurred in 2016, but it has had the most impact on public American sentiment. We chose this topic because awareness is the first step in making social change.
