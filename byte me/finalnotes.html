Final Notes


Future Plans

If we had more time, our web page would have included more data to add greater depth to our topic. We also would have expanded our topics to not only mass shootings, but mass shootings and shooting sprees. In addition, we would have fine tuned our website and made it more user friendly and aesthetically pleasing.

Lessons Learned

Over the course of constructing this web page, the data we have collected has debunked several racial stereotypes of mass shootings. For instance, nearly half of the perpetrators of the included shootings were white males. Furthermore, the risk of mass shootings is rising each year. If action isn't taken to prevent further tragedies, more lives will continue to be lost across America.
